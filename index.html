What is the Digital Literacy Course?

Our Digital Literacy Course is designed to help parents and older adults become digitally independent. From using social media to understanding mobile apps and online safety, we cover it all. Join us to bridge the generation gap with essential digital skills.

About Us

We are a team of dedicated professionals passionate about empowering older adults with digital skills. Our mission is to bridge the generation gap and make digital technology accessible to everyone.

What's in this Program?

  • Understanding Social Media and Fake News
  • Using Mobile Apps like Zomato, Ola, Google Maps, and PhonePe
  • Latest Trends & Tech Terms: Tweets, Memes, Virality
  • Online/Banking Fraud Prevention
  • Audio/Video Tutorials in Hindi & English
